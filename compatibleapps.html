What's Next for Simul?

We are working diligently on the next phase of Simul which will be to combine all dating Apps.

The Next Stage of Simul is to collaborate with dating Apps such as Bumble, Tinder, Grindr etc. to build a User Interface that allows a single Application for notifications.
Since most people nowadays date through applications such as these, we find that it would be ideal to have one place to go for everything. We are due to launch this portion in the coming months.
