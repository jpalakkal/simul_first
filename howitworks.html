How Simul Works

Simul is designed to work how you want it to work. It is completely customizable to fit your social media needs.Add any of the providers below to your feed and you will be able to see all your timelines in one place.

Simul works with Twitter to display your feed on your customizable homepage. See your notifications, check your direct messages, and the retweet function are all available through adding the application.



Simul also works with Instagram. By Adding the instagram app to your homepage, you can check your feed, see your likes, respond to direct messages, and creep ;)



Simul can work with Reddit as well. When Reddit is added to your feed you can see your feed, other user feeds and even run a multireddit which is multiple feeds at once. Reddit is widely used world wide and has many capabilities that can all be added to your homepage by a click of a button.



Simul also offers a collaboration with YouTube.YouTube users can apply your feed directly through the homepage by clicking YouTube in the Add function. This offers functions such as viewing your feed, seeing comments on your own videos.
